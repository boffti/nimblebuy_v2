Welcome to NimbleBuy

This is a humble effort to connect different regions in Bangalore to Vegetable vendors during the lockdown.

Start Shopping

How it works.

Request us to add your society/locality. Thereafter, you can browse the products and place an order.

We deliver every 2 days.

Once you place an order, the request is routed to the vendor in his vernacular. He sources the Veggies and Fruits and delivers them fresh to your society/locality.
